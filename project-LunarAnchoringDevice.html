Lunar Anchoring Mechanism

January 2022 - May 2022

Motivation & Objectives

The NASA Micro-g NExT program offers challenges for undergraduate students to design and manufacture a product to be tested by NASA. Our challenge was to fabricate a mechanism that can ultimately anchor to the surface of the moon while being lightweight, easy to handle, and following other requirements listed below.

Design Requirements (Completed)

  • Hold 10 lbs of force in any direction
  • Weigh less than 5 lbs
  • Fit within a 18" long and 6" diameter cylinder
  • Operate underwater
  • Used both by hand or robot
  • Not penetrate the surface of the rock nor chemically adhere to the surface
  • Not powered by external power or pressure
